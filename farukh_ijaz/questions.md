## Questions

Note that there may be other related questions along with the following:

1. How do you define ITS and how is ITS different from other fields of transportation engineering and planning?

2. Give us some examples of ITS where you have been part of the implementation?

3. In your experience, what has been the most fun ITS project you have worked on?

4. In your current role, what is your work day like? What is the favorite part of your work day?

5. What are the upcoming technologies in ITS in Canada?

6. What are the typical skills you'd need to be a good practioner in the field of ITS?

7. Do you think connected and autonomous vehicle technology will be implemented in most U.S. cities in the next few years (moving towards 100% autonomous private vehicles)? Do you believe in cities investing in those technologies or investing more in public transit?
